To Alisha,

I've written a hundred versions of this in my head. None of them do it justice, so here's the plain one.

You make hard days feel manageable and easy days feel like something worth celebrating. That's rarer than people admit, and I don't take it for granted.

I'm done treating "someday" like a placeholder. I know what I want, and it's a life built with you in it — properly, permanently, on purpose.
